Instructions

  1. Click SPEAK button to speak
  2. Click STOP to stop
  3. Say "color _____" to change the color of the circle. For example "color blue" to make the circle blue.
  4. Say "size _____" to change the size of the circle. For example "size 50" to make the circle with a diameter of 50. Only from 1 to 300.
  5. Say HELP for instructions.
